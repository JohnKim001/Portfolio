PROGRAMS

Kids Tiger Class [ages 3-6]

The Kids Tiger Program is designed for ages 3 to 6, crucial years in a child’s development. Students benefit from martial arts’ focus on building character, developing concentration and listening skills, learning respect for oneself and others, and improving self-esteem. The Kids Tiger works on a tipped belt system, which is ranked in the same order as a traditional belt order, but with a fun Kids Tiger class twist! Schedule your Free Trial Class today.

Adults Class [Ages 13+]

Adults class is a fun and challenging exercise routine as well as a great alternative to traditional gym workouts. Our classes build strength, endurance, flexibility, and coordination. Students learn about the history and philosophy of martial arts and grow to appreciate both its origins and meaning in their own lives. Regardless of whether you want to become more physically fit, increase flexibility, learn self-defense techniques, or engage in a fun and challenging activity with your kids, we have the program for you. The Adult program works on a traditional belt system with monthly belt testing opportunities. Schedule your Free Trial Class today.

Self-Defense Class [Ages 14+]

Self-Defense lessons is the best way to ensure your own personal safety. Students learn how to kick, punch, and block; how to defend against various punches, chokes, grabs, and take-downs; and how to deal with multiple attackers. The program also focuses on how to recognize potentially dangerous situations and respond appropriately. Self-Defense lesson is perfect for high school teenagers and college-bound students. Learning to protect oneself is a crucial part of growing up. College is an exciting and busy time, but more than anything else, it should be a positive and fulfilling experience. Most importantly, everyone should have the self-defense skills to make it safe. This program is open to ages. No previous martial arts experience is required.

ABOUT US

Our Mission

Kim's Martial Arts is dedicated to enhancing our community through martial arts training: Taekwondo, Hapkido, Weapons (Sword & Staff). Martial Arts is more than just punching and kicking, it is more about LIFE! We empower our students to have better health through physical training, as well as a vigorous mind through learning the positive mental principles: courtesy, integrity, perseverance, self-control, and indomitable spirit.
